Peanut Butter Milkshake!

It's sweet and a little salty, and definitely not something you get every day. It is so simple to make, so satisfying, and you probably already have everything you need to make this beautiful concoction.

  • Smooth peanut butter
  • Milk
  • Vanilla ice cream
  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place all the ingredients in the blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. We're done! Pour it up into a fancy glass and enjoy!
